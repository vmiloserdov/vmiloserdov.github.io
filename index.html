About Me

My name is Vlad Miloserdov and I am an undergraduate student at Carnegie Mellon University, pursuing degrees in Computer Science and Logic. As a research assistant at DDM Lab, I contributed to the development of advanced simulations designed to model and counter network threats. My work aims to enhance strategic decision-making in complex cyber environments, advancing the field of digital defense. My research interests lie in building multi-agent environments that optimize coordination and response in dynamic network settings.
